Instructor: Samson Zhou
Lectures: MWF 08/21/2023-12/12/2023, 1:50-2:40 PM CT, HRBB 126

Course Description

Algorithms for data science; sublinear algorithms; models of computation for big data; differential privacy.

Grading

Scribe notes, i.e., succinct LaTeX summary of lectures 20%, midterm presentation 35%, final project 45%

Course Schedule

  • Week 1: Probability basics (hashing, birthday paradox, coupon-collector, balls-in-bins, power-of-two choices)
  • Week 2: Probability basics continued, concentration inequalities (Markov, Chebyshev, Chernoff)
  • Week 3: Dimensionality reduction (Johnson-Lindenstrauss)
  • Week 4: Streaming model (reservoir sampling, norm estimation, heavy-hitters, distinct elements)
  • Week 5: Sliding window model, adversarial robustness
  • Week 6: Differential privacy
  • Week 7: Reading paper presentations
  • Week 8: Clustering in the streaming model, coresets
  • Week 9: Numerical linear algebra in the streaming model (Linear regression, subspace embeddings, low-rank approximation
  • Week 10: Online learning, linear programming, semi-definite programming
  • Week 11: Learning-augmented algorithms
  • Week 12: Optional topics
  • Week 13: Optional topics
  • Week 14: Optional topics
  • Week 15: Project presentations
